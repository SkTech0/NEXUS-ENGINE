## Abstract

— **Purpose**: One sentence on the goal.  
— **Design/Methodology**: Brief description of approach (e.g., system design, experiments).  
— **Findings**: Main results in 2–3 sentences.  
— **Originality/Value**: What is new and why it matters.

**Index Terms** — Term1, Term2, Term3, Term4, Term5 (5–7 terms).

**Word count**: Abstract typically 150–250 words.
