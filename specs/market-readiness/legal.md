## 1. Purpose

This document defines legal readiness for NEXUS market entry: terms of service, privacy, liability, IP, and contractual requirements. Legal readiness is required for commercial launch and enterprise contracts.

---

## 2. Legal Readiness Criteria

### 2.1 Terms of Service and Usage

- **Terms of Service (ToS):** ToS or equivalent governing use of platform/API; acceptance flow and versioning of terms.
- **Acceptable use:** Acceptable use policy (AUP) where applicable; prohibited uses and consequences.
- **SLA and limitations:** SLA commitments (availability, latency) and limitations of liability; exclusion of consequential damages where permitted by law.
- **Support terms:** Support tiers and response times; escalation and escalation SLA.

### 2.2 Privacy and Data Protection

- **Privacy policy:** Privacy policy describing collection, use, retention, and sharing of data; consent where required (e.g., GDPR).
- **Data processing:** Data processing agreement (DPA) or equivalent for B2B; processor obligations and sub-processor disclosure.
- **Data location and transfer:** Data residency and cross-border transfer (e.g., SCCs, adequacy); documented for customers and regulators.
- **Data subject rights:** Process for data subject access, rectification, erasure, portability; response SLA where required.

### 2.3 Liability and Indemnity

- **Liability cap:** Liability cap in ToS or order form; alignment with insurance and risk.
- **Indemnity:** Indemnity for IP infringement and (where applicable) data breach or regulatory fines; scope and cap documented.
- **Insurance:** Insurance (e.g., cyber, E&O) in place for commercial launch; limits per risk and customer requirement.

### 2.4 Intellectual Property

- **IP ownership:** Ownership of platform IP; customer data and customer-generated content remain customer IP.
- **Licenses:** License to use platform/API (e.g., limited, non-exclusive, revocable); restrictions (e.g., no resale, no reverse engineering).
- **Open source:** Open source components and licenses; compliance and attribution; no GPL or similar copyleft in critical path unless intentional.

### 2.5 Contractual Readiness

- **Order form / MSA:** Master service agreement (MSA) or order form for commercial customers; scope, pricing, SLA, and term.
- **DPA and security addendum:** DPA and security addendum (or security exhibit) for enterprise; audit rights, breach notification.
- **Vendor and partner agreements:** Vendor and partner agreements (e.g., cloud, data) in place; flow-down of obligations where required.
