# Nexus Playground

**Product demo & exploration platform for decision intelligence.**

---

## What is Nexus Playground?

Nexus Playground is a **product** that lets engineers, investors, clients, and stakeholders experience the Nexus decision intelligence platform without touching production systems. It is the face of “what the platform can do”—a safe, curated environment for demos and exploration.

## Who is it for?

- **Engineers** exploring capabilities and building proofs of concept  
- **Investors** evaluating the platform’s value  
- **Clients** seeing decision intelligence in action before adoption  
- **Demos** for sales, conferences, and partnerships  

## What problem does it solve?

- “How do we show decision intelligence without risking real data or production?”  
- “Where can we try scenarios before committing to a product?”  
- “How do we tell the platform story in 15 minutes?”  

## Why it exists

Playground exists to **demonstrate value** and **reduce adoption friction**. It answers “what can this do?” and “how would it work for us?” in a low-risk, high-clarity way.

## Value

- **Trust**: See the platform work before buying or integrating.  
- **Clarity**: One place for the “platform story” and hands-on exploration.  
- **Speed**: Fast path from “interested” to “convinced.”  

## What belongs here (product scope)

- Demo flows and scenarios  
- Sample data and example decisions  
- Exploration UI and guided tours  
- Product narrative and positioning  

## What does NOT belong here

- Production loan/hiring/fraud decisions  
- Real customer data  
- Billing, entitlements, or tenant management  
- Core platform engine code  

---

*This directory defines the **product** Nexus Playground. Platform implementation lives elsewhere; this is the product identity, vision, and scope.*
