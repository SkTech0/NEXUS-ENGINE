# Nexus Hiring Engine — User Personas

---

## 1. The candidate

- **Goal**: Be evaluated fairly and (where possible) understand the outcome.  
- **Needs**: Clear shortlist/interview/hire (or equivalent), reason or feedback if in scope.  
- **Behavior**: Applies via ATS or employer; may request feedback or appeal if allowed.  
- **Success**: Receives a fair, understandable evaluation.  

## 2. The hiring manager

- **Goal**: Get consistent, explainable recommendations for who to shortlist or hire.  
- **Needs**: Recommendation per candidate, reason (fit/gaps), optional comparison view.  
- **Behavior**: Reviews recommendations, may override or refer; uses product in hiring flow.  
- **Success**: Faster, more consistent shortlisting and hiring decisions.  

## 3. The HR / talent lead

- **Goal**: Standardize hiring decisions across teams and reduce bias.  
- **Needs**: Configurable criteria, referral rules, fairness and audit views.  
- **Behavior**: Configures product, monitors usage, handles appeals or overrides.  
- **Success**: Hiring is consistent and auditable across the organization.  

## 4. The integration / tech owner (enterprise)

- **Goal**: Embed Hiring Engine into ATS or HR workflow with minimal friction.  
- **Needs**: Clear integration contract, docs, and support; stable behavior.  
- **Behavior**: Integrates API, tests flows, monitors in production.  
- **Success**: Hiring Engine is live in their flow with stable behavior.  

## 5. The auditor / compliance

- **Goal**: Verify that hiring decisions are fair, consistent, and explainable.  
- **Needs**: Audit trail, explanation per recommendation, criteria visibility (as allowed).  
- **Behavior**: Reviews reports, samples, or export; may ask for specific decision trace.  
- **Success**: Can attest that the product supports fairness and compliance.  

---

*Personas define who the product serves. They inform flows, features, and prioritization—not implementation details.*
